Community + Clinical Partners

For Partners

If you run a nonprofit, lead a clinical team, or work with pediatric populations in San Diego: this page is for you.

Who I'm looking for

Two kinds of partners

Clinical and hospital partners
Social work teams, specialty clinics, discharge coordinators

I'm looking for clinical teams (primarily at Rady Children's Hospital) who want a structured student navigator resource embedded in their existing workflow. The PNS model is built around your referral pipeline: you identify families who need post-discharge follow-up support, I contact them, run the intake, and work cases until closure.

I'm also looking for specialty clinic partnerships for AHI's navigator programs (SDOH screening in hem/onc, sickle cell, LGBTQ care, and care transitions). These are longer-term relationships with a research component.

Community organizations
Nonprofits, FQHCs, health advocacy orgs

SDIPP is built for community-embedded programming. We bring student volunteers to your venue and your population. If you work with seniors, youth, or underserved communities in San Diego and want an evidence-based injury prevention or health literacy program, SDIPP has the infrastructure.

AHI partnerships with community organizations focus on pediatric populations: organizations serving LGBTQ youth, immigrant families, families navigating chronic illness, or anyone in the gap between a clinical referral and actually getting to care.

What we bring

What a partnership looks like

Student navigators
Trained pre-medical students with structured protocols, defined scope of practice, and consistent follow-through. They make calls, solve problems, and close cases.
SDOH screening
Systematic social determinants intake at the family level: transportation, insurance, housing, food security, language, childcare. Documented and acted on.
Program delivery
SDIPP has delivered hands-on programming at senior facilities, schools, and community health fairs across San Diego. Flexible on format; non-negotiable on the hands-on, evidence-based standard.
Research output
Every AHI project includes a data collection and evaluation component. Partners who contribute access to populations contribute to publishable research. That's the explicit goal.

Next step

Let's talk

The best first step is a 20-to-30-minute call to understand your population, your current gaps, and whether there's a genuine fit. I'm not looking to pitch a predetermined solution; I want to understand what you're working with and see if what I'm building is actually useful.

Email me to schedule. Include a sentence about your organization and what you're working on. I'll respond within a day or two.
